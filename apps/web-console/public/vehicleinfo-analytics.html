Metric Calculation Logic
Metric Formula Source
Total Revenue Challan revenue + Service history revenue + FASTag revenue + Insurance revenue DuckDB all service tables + policybazaar_*_daily
Total Spend vendor_payouts + gateway_fees + user_refunds + ad_spend (manual/CRM) RDS payment tables + manual config
Net Profit Total Revenue − Total Spend Computed
ROI Total Revenue ÷ Total Spend Computed
Challan Revenue SUM(convenience_fee + gst) WHERE payment_date BETWEEN start AND end RDS challan_payment table
Service History Revenue SUM(agent_fee + gateway_fee − refund) WHERE created_at BETWEEN dates RDS service_history_payments table
FASTag Revenue SUM(amount) WHERE status='captured' AND created_at BETWEEN dates RDS buy_fastag_payment table
Insurance Revenue (Bike) SUM(bike_sale_count) × ₹200 WHERE report_date BETWEEN dates DuckDB policybazaar_bike_daily
Insurance Revenue (Car) SUM(car_sale_count) × ₹600 WHERE report_date BETWEEN dates DuckDB policybazaar_car_daily
Insurance Revenue (CV) SUM(cv_sale_count) × ₹1200 WHERE report_date BETWEEN dates DuckDB policybazaar_cv_daily
Challan CAC challan_ad_spend ÷ challan_order_count Config + DuckDB
ROI (revenue efficiency) Total Revenue ÷ Total Ad Spend Computed
DB Fields Required per Section
Section Table / Source Key Fields

CV Insurance DuckDB → policybazaar_cv_daily report_date, cv_sale_count, revenue
